Dinner Bell IX: Holiday Edition

A Culinary and Musical Journey

Sunday, December 9th, 2018

Featuring

Chef Anne Quatrano

Chef Anne Quatrano

A James Beard award winner and longtime proponent of sustainability, Quatrano prides herself in using locally grown seasonal and organic produce, much of which is from her own organic gardens. She now operates some of Atlanta’s most celebrated restaurants: Bacchanalia, Floataway Café, and Star Provisions.

Musical Guest

John Driskell Hopkins

John Driskell Hopkins

John Driskell Hopkins is a founding member, vocalist, multi-instrumentalist and songwriter for the Grammy Award Winning group Zac Brown Band.

In 1926, a group of chefs and musicians in Atlanta gathered in the tower of the Sears & Roebuck building in Atlanta, GA to put on a radio broadcast called Dinner Bell; it lasted only two years.

In the spirit of reviving this great collaboration, We've set the table, bringing together southern restauranteurs, incredible chefs and influential musicians to help promote sustainable foods and local farms in the southeast. We cordially invite you to join us on Sunday September 30th 2018 at the lovely new Bacchanalia to celebrate song and food with acclaimed Chef Anne Quatrano and musical guest John Driskell Hopkins of Zac Brown Band.

Seating is limited. $135 Ticket includes live music and dinner (alcohol not included).

This event is pre-sale only. No tickets available at the door. Dress is casual. Doors open at 6:30pm and dinner will be served promptly at 7:00pm, please be on time.
